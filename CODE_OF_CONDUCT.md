# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge to create an environment where all participants feel safe, respected, and valued, regardless of background, identity, or experience level. We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or any behavior that undermines the dignity or well-being of our community members.

## Standard

looptrip adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
